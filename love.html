A love letter, of sorts

I have spent innumerable hours thinking of what to do. How to show the love I hold for you. How to let you feel the love I have for you. And even now, it feels like it's not enough.

I love you, my hubby.
I love you more than I could ever imagine.
I love you more than I ever thought possible.
I love you so much that I forgot what it's like to not love you.
My love, my star, my soul, my life. The love of my life, my lovey, my husband.
I love you all of the much.

And I don't know if this will be enough. I don't even know if it'll be decent. But I'm doing my best to show you just how much I love you. And I truly wish I could give you everything you deserve. This, me, this life. You deserve so much more. And I will do my best everyday to grant you that and so much more. Because I love you. And I love being loved by you.
